Your survey responses drive everything. AI is part of the workflow — but guided by your specific inputs, industry context, and marketing best practices, and reviewed before delivery. This is not generic prompt output. It's a system designed to produce content that fits your business.
Can I choose which platforms?
Yes. Pick any two from Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, TikTok, Pinterest, or YouTube. The captions are written to match each platform's format and tone.
Are images or graphics included?
No — you'll use your own visuals. But every caption comes with a visual pairing suggestion so you know exactly what kind of image or video to pair with it.
How long do I have to fill out the survey?
You have 30 days from purchase to submit your survey. After that the link expires. Most people fill it out within a day or two.
Can I reorder next month?
Yes — and many customers do. There's no subscription. Just come back when you need a new drop. Same simple process every time.
What's your refund policy?
All sales are final on custom digital products. That said, if there's ever an issue with your delivery — a missing caption, incorrect platform, or anything that doesn't match your order — reach out to Elizabeth directly and we'll fix it. Every deliverable is reviewed before it reaches you.
