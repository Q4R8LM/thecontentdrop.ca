The Origin

Most business owners know they need to post. They just don't know what to say.

Hi — I'm Elizabeth. I've spent 15 years working in professional marketing, and the same pattern kept showing up everywhere I looked. Small business owners knew they needed to show up online. They just never knew what to say — so they didn't. And the longer they left it, the harder it got to start.

The solution wasn't another template pack or a generic content calendar. It was a system that actually listened to the business, understood its audience, and produced real content that could be used immediately. No calls. No agency overhead. No ongoing commitment.

The Content Drop is that system. I built it so that any business owner — regardless of budget or marketing experience — could access the kind of content strategy that actually works. You answer a short survey. I build your content. You post consistently. That's it.

— Elizabeth, Founder of The Content Drop

  • Custom over generic

    No discovery calls. No onboarding sessions. No project timelines. Buy, fill out a survey, receive your content. That's the whole process.

    Consistent posting beats occasional perfection every time. The goal is to give you content you can actually use — quickly, reliably, and without friction.
